Contact

If you want to get in touch with us, feel free to send us a message via our email coreteam [at] nushackers [fullstop] org.

This is ideal for things like: you’d like to request a workshop, you’d like to do a guest post on our site, you have further questions about our membership and organization. For general discussion, or to interact with community members, we recommend you go to our Discussion page.

Note: If you’re about to contact us to 'hack' into a system, don’t bother: we’ll report you to the relevant authorities, and may choose to shame you on our blog. We're a hacker organization, not a cracker organization. Know the difference.
